## Must-Do Experiences

### Bucket List Activities
1. **Canoe Breakfast** - Delivered by traditional outrigger canoe ($75-$150)
2. **Shark & Ray Snorkeling Safari** - 3-hour lagoon tour ($86+/person)
3. **Private Motu Dinner** - Champagne sunset dinner (~$1,000/couple at Four Seasons)
4. **Jet Ski Tour** - Explore hidden coves ($250-$400)
5. **Helicopter Tour** - Aerial views of Mt. Otemanu ($400-$600)
6. **Glass Floor Stargazing** - Free with overwater bungalow

### Best Photo Opportunities
- The Swing at InterContinental Thalasso (10am-12pm)
- Motu Tapu from Conrad
- Mt. Otemanu view from your bungalow deck
- Sunrise/sunset from private terrace

## Booking Tips for May 2026

1. **Book 6+ months in advance** - High season fills quickly
2. **Check renovation schedules** - Conrad and Thalasso both have work planned
3. **Consider loyalty programs:**
   - Marriott Bonvoy (St. Regis, Westin)
   - Hilton Honors (Conrad)
   - IHG Rewards (InterContinental)
4. **Look for package deals** - Air Tahiti Nui packages can save money
5. **Request Mt. Otemanu-facing rooms** - The iconic Bora Bora view
6. **Tuesday/Wednesday/Saturday flights** - Often cheapest
